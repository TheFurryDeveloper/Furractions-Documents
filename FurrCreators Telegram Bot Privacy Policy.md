FurrCreators (“the Bot”) is a Telegram bot owned and operated by Furractions. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, how it is stored, and your rights regarding your data when interacting with the Bot.

By using the Bot, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect
We collect only the minimum information required for the Bot to function properly. This may include:
Your Telegram user ID (required for identifying your account within the Bot system)
Your Telegram username (only if available through Telegram)
Bot usage status (whether you have interacted with or used the Bot)
Basic operational metadata required for functionality (such as command triggers)

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial information, private messages, or device-level data.

We do not access, read, or store the full content of your Telegram messages beyond what is strictly required for the Bot to process commands and respond correctly.

How We Use Your Information
Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:
Providing core Bot functionality
Identifying users within the Bot system
Processing commands and generating responses
Maintaining Bot stability, performance, and reliability
Preventing abuse, spam, or malicious usage

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial gain.
